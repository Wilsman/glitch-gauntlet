# Glitch Gauntlet: Gameplay Guide

## üïπÔ∏è Core Mechanics
Players must survive increasingly difficult waves of enemies. Combat is **auto-attacking**; players focus on positioning and dodging while their character automatically targets the nearest threat.

### Waves & Progression
- **Waves**: Each wave lasts for a set duration.
- **Enemies**: Killing enemies drops XP orbs.
- **Leveling**: Collecting XP fills the level bar. Upon leveling up, players choose from 3 random upgrades.
- **Hellhound Rounds**: Special challenge rounds occur every 5 waves (Waves 5, 10, etc.), featuring packs of fast-moving "Hellhounds".
- **Boss Fights**: Confront powerful bosses with unique mechanics (Berserker, Summoner, Architect).

## üé≠ Character System
Each character has a unique weapon and base stats:
- **Spray-n-Pray**: High attack speed, lower damage.
- **Boom-Bringer**: Grenade launcher with AoE damage.
- **Glass-Cannon-Carl**: High damage, very low health.
- **Pet-Pal-Percy**: Starts with a loyal combat pet.
- **Vampire-Vex**: Life-steal focused gameplay.
- **Turret-Tina**: Master of static defenses.
- **Dash-Dynamo**: High mobility and blinking.
